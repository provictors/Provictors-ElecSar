FEATURES

  ElecSaR is an electricity saving reminder connected to the main electric board and an app.

  It has a tricolour display on the mobile application giving visual indication of power consumption range- Green, Yellow  and Red for low, moderate and high respectively.

  After calculating the increase in meter reading every ten minutes, the mobile application displays the range in which the  energy consumption for that interval lied in. It notifies when the consumption is in the red range.

  In each device purchased by any household, industry, etc, the high, moderate and low ranges are set based on the  energy load sanctioned to the establishment.

  Arduino techonology will be used to program the device and connect ElecSaR to the mobile application. Ammeter and  other devices will be used to calculate the energy consumption every ten minutes and per month.

  Manufacturing cost of ElecSaR per device is Rs. 500-550 . (Printed circuit board (by using 3D printers) ,bluetooth device,  ElecSaR APP, leads etc.)

 Our device, Elecsar provides you with the following features:

 1) Measures and displays the electricity consumed every month.

 2) Shows electricity consumed every ten minutes and indicates whether it is in the ideal range set, according to the  sanction load subscribed by the user.

 3) An emergency button for switching off mains.



 The device has been programmed in three ways:

 1) Firstly, when a customer applies for our product we will set the three ranges based on the sanctioned load subscribed by the customer. Incase the ranges have to be modified, the customer can apply for modification.

 2) Secondly, it takes the reading on first day of every month and then shows the difference in the reading. Then it displays  whether this consumption was within the ideal range set or not. Incase it was in the Red range(high), it notifies on the  mobile application.

 3) Thirdly, it takes the reading every 10 minutes and finds the difference in reading. Again, it displays whether the  consumption fell within green/yellow/red range and notifies if electricity consumption was high(Red range).
